Techpro DC Announces Distribution Agreement with CrowdStrike

#newpartner
#crowdstrike

We are trilled to announce at GITEX GLOBAL 2024 the signing of a distribution agreement with CrowdStrike to protect regional businesses with industry-leading AI-native cybersecurity.

The new partnership will allow Techpro DC to offer its customers and partners the CrowdStrike Falcon® cybersecurity platform to drive vendor consolidation and stop breaches. Techpro DC will sell the Falcon platform across Central Asia, including Azerbaijan, Georgia, Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, and Turkmenistan, in its operational region. This will address the diverse needs of enterprises and help ensure they operate in secure, reliable IT environments, protected against modern cyber threats.

CrowdStrike is headquartered in the United States and operates globally. It is one of the most innovative companies in the security sector worldwide, providing AI-native protection across critical areas of enterprise risk – endpoints and cloud workloads, identity and data.
