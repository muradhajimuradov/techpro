Our Story

Founded in 2013, Techpro DC is one of the largest broad-line IT-distributors in Transcaucasia (Azerbaijan, Georgia) and Central Asia countries (Uzbekistan, Turkmenistan and Tajikistan). Techpro DC supplies products of world’s leading manufacturers as Hewlett Packard Enterprise, Hewlett Packard Incorporated, DELL Technologies, Aruba Networks, Micro Focus, Radware, Kingston, Apacer, Radiflow, QNAP, Fudo and etc. The Company’s dealer network numbers more than 300 partners from Transcaucasia (Azerbaijan, Georgia) and Central Asia countries (Uzbekistan, Turkmenistan and Tajikistan) countries. Principles of responsible approach to work, trust and strong relationships with partners in the market contributes to the fact that we always take part in various tenders in the field of IT and hand over huge projects.  One of the advantages of Techpro DC is its warehouses in Azerbaijan, Georgia and in Uzbekistan. Highly skilled personnel, modern technique and solutions, professional and responsible approach to the clients’ requirements, high quality of the services offered are the main features distinguishing Techpro DC among others.
